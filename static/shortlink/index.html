Export / Import, drag-to-reorder & dynamic links

Export downloads your shortlinks as shortlinks.json — back them up or move them to another browser. Import merges a shortlinks.json or your browser's exported bookmarks.html (Bookmark manager → Export bookmarks). Drag a link to reorder it — drop it on another group's links or header to move it there. Dynamic: #name/rest appends rest to the target, and a {q} in a target is replaced by it (search engines).
